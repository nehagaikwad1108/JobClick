WEBINAR: Business intelligence and advanced analytics

Webinar recorded on : May 23, 2018

Duration: 60 min


WEBINAR DESCRIPTION:

Why is your data important? Having an agile, modern Business Intelligence & Advanced Analytics platform enables fast & accurate access to critical insights across a range of analytics. Organizations in the global trade era must quickly and successfully navigate supply chains, converting data to intelligence, providing fast answers to complicated questions. Smarter decisions can be made faster by using quickly accessed data (seconds versus days, reducing margin of error by having more accurate data). Business Intelligence & Advanced Analytics is not new, but what is new is the speed of the information as well as the detail of the data to new levels by providing filtered aspects of the business.


Reasons to attend the webinar:

- Learn new methods of utilizing Big Data 

- Learn what leading organizations are doing to leverage Business Intelligence 

- Learn how modern Supply Chains are harnessing the power of Big Data to better serve their customers 


SPEAKERS:
