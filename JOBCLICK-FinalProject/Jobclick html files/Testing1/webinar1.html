WEBINAR: User onboarding emails that drive customer success and free-to-paid conversations

Webinar recorded on : August 16, 2018

Duration: 60 min


WEBINAR DESCRIPTION:

It’s time to dust off your user onboarding emails and make them your highest converting channel. Chameleon and Sendwithus are excited to host Alli Blum, a SaaS onboarding expert and conversion copywriter who will share her strategies for designing an onboarding email sequence that drives more paying customers.


In this session you'll learn:

- How your email strategy helps drive conversion

- How to design your onboarding sequences

- Copywriting techniques that encourage users to take action

- BONUS: Live onboarding email teardown 


SPEAKERS:

Alli Blum, SaaS Onboarding Consultant and Conversion CopywriterAlli Blum is dedicated to helping SaaS businesses keep more of their trial users by removing the “hidden work” from onboarding—and replacing it with conversion-optimized web and email copy. She has helped technology businesses talk to customers for 8 years. Her published writing has appeared on industry publications like Kissmetrics, Autopilot, Mixpanel, and Crazy Egg.
